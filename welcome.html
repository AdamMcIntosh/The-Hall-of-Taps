Welcome to the Hall of Taps

Welcome, to what I believe is the third iteration of the Hall of Taps. You can view all the beers and their hall rating right here.

Hopefully, this time I stay committed to blogging regularly and updating weekly the Hall and Ratings.

I also have all the Breweries with Rated Beers here (use the brewery filter on the beers page). The goal is to keep this updated regularly. I used to write at baseball (horribly), and I frequent a site called FanGraphs. FanGraphs led me to a site called Beergraphs. Which is no more, so I thought I'd keep something in the spirit of it going.

The the Hall of Stats also provided inspiration for this site. In the past this site has been powered using PHP & MySQL, as well as using C# and MSSQL. This time around I'm using GitHub Pages. You can get all the data here. As time goes on I plan on making the data better searchable, so bear with me for now. It's a work in progress, but the journey is going to be fun (I hope).
